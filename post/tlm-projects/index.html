TLM Projects

2017/10/01

Volunteering with Yayasan Tanaoba Lais Manekat (TLM - We Serve With Love) in Kupang, West Timor, Indonesia, my role was as clean water advisor, capacity building WASH principles to local Kupang based staff, the field facilitators and communities.

http://tlmfoundation.or.id/en/home-page/

Whilst in Timor we worked mainly with three villages: Nekmese, Kuibaat, Maubesi, and Lidamanu.
