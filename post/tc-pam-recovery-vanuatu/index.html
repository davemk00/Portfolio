TC Pam Recovery - Vanuatu

2016/03/01

A two month mission with the Vanuatu Red Cross for WASH assessment on TC Pam Recovery. Designed spring capture water supply systems and completed four day needs assessment.

On the 13th March 2015, Tropical Cyclone Pam hit the northern and central islands of Vanuatu. Continuing into March 14th, the cyclone travelled south through the archipelago causing further massive devastation in the southern islands of Vanuatu. In total 188,000 people were affected by Cyclone Pam, approximately 70% of Vanuatu’s total population 1
